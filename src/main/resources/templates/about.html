Our Story

Elite Appliance was founded in 1985 by Johnathan Clarke, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for innovative home solutions. Starting as a modest family business in a small warehouse, Elite Appliance has grown into a leading name in the appliance industry, known for its commitment to quality, service, and technological advancement.
